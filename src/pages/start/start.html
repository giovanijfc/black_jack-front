How it works?

The dealer gives two starting cards. At this point the player can choose to be alone with these two cards.

At any time he can choose hit (pick up one more card) or stand (stop).

If the player has cards with an added value greater than 21, he automatically loses.

If the cards are 21, he wins automatically.

If they add up to less than 21, the dealer picks up cards until he gets at least sum 17; the player wins if his hand adds more than the dealer's or if the dealer's hand exceeds 21 points. AZ is worth 11 only 11!
